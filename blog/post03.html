Typeface Choose

May 15th, 2017

I use the “Anton” as my portal page header font. The typeface is very clearly. And I like it use as bold display font in my web titles. “Anton” was designed by Vernon, a lifelong artist, graduated with an MA in Typeface Design from the University of Reading. The typeface was extended from a single bold style into a family in March 2013. It is very popular in US and Japan. After researched online I found the website “bigstorageworks” use this typeface. I don’t think they use it properly. The website uses too much different font in this webpage. And there is a little bit difficulty to watch clearly what they hope the user to read. The important reason I like this typeface is it bold enough to be a header, and it very clearly as a title.
